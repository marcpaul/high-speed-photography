The Concept

The basic concept is that the flash unit is fired when the object interruptes the laser beam.
So you can capture opjects in motion for example.

In detail:
  • It must be dark.
  • When you switch on SW1 you can adjust the laser and sensor.
    LED1 is on if laser hits sensor.
    After adjusting laser switch SW1 off.
  • With ROT1 you can set a delay.
    When playing around with the setup you will understand why.
  • Now you press BUT1 and the camera is triggered.
    You must set the shutter speed to a high value.
  • Then you're waiting...
    If the laser beam is interrupted the flash unit fires.
    That's it.

The Texas Instruments MSP430G2452 Microcontroller does all the work.
The Opto Coupler is very important because the flash unit needs a high current.
The Opto Coupler isolates the flash circuit and is triggered with the MSP MC.
